RPO AI Acceleration Program

Full Training Curriculum

Welcome to the complete training portal for the RPO AI Acceleration Program. The program is divided into three distinct phases. Select a session from any module below to begin.

Executive Summary

  • Phase 1 builds AI foundations for all team members.
  • Phase 2 offers optional advanced training for champions.
  • Phase 3 focuses on strategic leadership and measurable impact.
PHASE 1: MANDATORY

AI Foundations

This foundational phase is designed for all team members. In just two weeks, everyone will become comfortable and proficient with using AI for their core daily tasks, from writing emails to sourcing candidates.

  • Gain confidence with AI in two weeks.
  • Apply AI to emails, sourcing, and daily work.

MODULE 1

Prompting & Writing — The C.R.E.A.T.E. Framework

Learn the C.R.E.A.T.E. framework — a structured approach (Character, Request, Examples, Additions, Type, Extras) for crafting precise, repeatable, high-quality prompts.

MODULE 2

Sourcing & Research

Generate complex Boolean strings and perform rapid market analysis.

PHASE 2: OPTIONAL

The AI Champions Track

This track is for team members who want to go deeper. Champions will learn advanced skills like data analysis, workflow automation, and how to build custom AI tools, positioning them as mentors and subject matter experts.

  • Develop advanced data and automation skills.
  • Build custom tools and mentor teammates.

MODULE 3

Data & Knowledge

Analyze data in Sheets and build a "CV Bot" to search documents.

MODULE 4

Automation

Build hands-free workflows that connect Gmail, AI, and Sheets.

MODULE 5

Train the Trainer

Learn to mentor colleagues and present your projects to leadership.

PHASE 3: LEADERSHIP

AI for RPO Strategists

This executive stream is for Department Heads and Team Leads. The focus shifts from hands-on skills to strategic planning, governance, and measuring the business value and ROI of AI initiatives.

  • Design governance and roadmaps for AI initiatives.
  • Measure ROI and business value from AI projects.

MODULE 6

Strategy & Governance

Develop an RPO-specific AI roadmap and prioritize high-impact projects.

MODULE 7

Measuring Impact

Define success metrics and build a compelling business case for AI projects.
